FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about short-term loans.

What is the penalty for missing a repayment?
Additional interest will be charged on a pro-rata basis when missing a monthly repayment. We will contact you immediately to arrange a solution. Early communication is key - please contact us before missing a payment.
What are the repayment terms available?
We offer flexible repayment terms from 1 to 6 months. You can choose the term that works best for your budget. All terms come with our fixed 5% interest rate.
What is the interest rate?
We offer a fixed interest rate of 5% on all short-term loans, regardless of the repayment term you choose. This rate is transparent and clearly disclosed before you sign any agreement. No hidden fees or surprises.
How long does it take to receive my cash loan?
Once approved, funds are paid into your bank account within 24 hours. Many customers receive money the same day.
What documents do I need to apply?
You'll need: valid SA ID, proof of residence (not older than 3 months), latest payslip, and 3 months bank statements.
